How to Toast Bread

What is Bread?

Bread is a marvelous invention of humanity which is created by dough and flour. This amazing creation can be further enhanced when toasted. Toast, not be mistaken with the restaurant point of sale and management system, is sliced bread which has been browned by heat. Below are the necessary equipment and step-by-step instructions on how to toast bread.

What do I need?

  • Bread
  • Knife
  • Electricity
  • Toaster

What do I do?

  1. Slice the bread
  2. Place bread in the toaster
  3. Adjust toaster settings to taste
  4. Activate the toaster and wait
    5. You've created toast!
