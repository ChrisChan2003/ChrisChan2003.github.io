## About accessibility
**Accessibility** as defined in this document refers to how acclimmated something is to the needs to every user. It is important to consider accessibility when creating a product for general audiences. Accessibility is not about shaping the world or a product to specifically meet the needs of people with disabilities, but instead it seeks to create something that can be used comfortably by *anybody* despite any differences in ability, race, or any of the other ways we all differ. Creating accessible products ensures that it reaches as many people as possible while remaining comfortable and inclusive to any user. :smiley:
